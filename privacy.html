PRIVACY POLICY

What is GDPR ?

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679 is a regulation in EU law on data protection and privacy for all individuals within the European Union (EU) and the European Economic Area (EEA). It also addresses the export of personal data outside the EU and EEA.Our privacy policy is in accordance to the GDPR so that we protect your information the best when you use our website.

This information includes your name(first and last name),address,phone number, e-mail address,financial and credit card information, order details and other information that you provide us when making purchases, subscribing to our newsletter,subscribing to be kept up to date, reporting a problem, writing a review, sending us correspondence or any details you provide whaen registering to use our site.

Information we collect about you:-

To identify areas of improvement we collect information automatically about your visit during your time on our website referred to as "cookies".

We may combine this information with information you give us and information we collect about you to give you the best service experience. However, certain third-party service providers, such as payment gateways and other payment transaction processors have their own privacy policies in respect to the information we are required to provide to them for your purchase-related transactions.

For these providers, we recommend that you read their privacy policies so you can understand the manner in which your personal details are handled by these providers.
