Benifts & Earn

Rewards Program

Every one pound you spend you earn 100. Points validity can vary from a minimum of 12 months to a maximum of 18 months from the date of earning. Points are expired in 2 cycles each year.

How to manage ?

To manage your rewards program account online, click on “My Rewards” under the Rewards Program tab on the homepage on www.noshahi.com or Rewards Program in the Mobile App Menu on the App homepage. You can edit profile information, view current points balance & last ten purchases history.

Hence, it’s essential that your contact details are updated with us to receive transactional & promotional communication.

Earning points

Earn points every time you make a purchase at a Noshahi Ltd or on www.noshahi.com or noshahi Mobile App. The more you purchase, the more points you accumulate. On crossing purchase slabs (defined in the table below), you start receiving an even higher number of points. You also get up to 100 bonus points on updating your profile, including bonus points on shopping during your birthday week, provided you have shared your date of birth. Also, you would be entitled to 100 bonus points on your 2nd transaction and 200 bonus points on tier up gradation. We treat your email as your loyalty number so that it’s easy to remember. Please quote your mobile number at the time of billing. All point details and statements will be sent as text messages on your mobile periodically.

Redeeming Points

You need a minimum of 5000 points, the first time, to start redeeming (5000 points = spends worth £5.00). After that, you can redeem as many points you have in your account. To redeem, visit login to your account on www.noshahi.com or noshahi mobile app and exchange desired points for products or applicable redemption offers.
